About PassManNNU

What is PassManNNU?

PassManNNU is a secure, full-stack web-based password manager designed as part of a university cybersecurity course project. It enables users to securely store, manage, and retrieve their credentials — including website names, usernames, emails, and passwords — all within an encrypted digital vault.

Our Mission

Our mission is to demonstrate that strong cybersecurity can coexist with elegant, user-friendly design. We aim to provide a practical, real-world example of how modern web applications should handle sensitive data, following industry standards for encryption, authentication, and access control.

Security Best Practices

PassManNNU is built with multiple layers of security:

  • AES Encryption (Fernet) — All vault passwords are encrypted at rest
  • PBKDF2 Hashing — Account passwords and passkeys are securely hashed
  • CSRF Protection — All forms are protected against cross-site request forgery
  • Session Management — Auto-logout after 15 minutes of inactivity
  • Rate Limiting — Login attempts are limited to prevent brute-force attacks
